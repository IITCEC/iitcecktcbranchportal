About IIT-CEC, Katlicherra Branch


The IIT-CEC Katlicherra Branch, located on College Road opposite the Circle Office in Katlicherra, Hailakandi, Assam (PIN 788161), is a prominent centre under The Institute of Information Technology Computer Education Centre (IIT-CEC). Led by Center Director Mr. Raj Kumar Bhowmik, this branch is part of one of the oldest and largest IT education networks in Northeast India .


Certificates, Registration and Courses

IIT-CEC is an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution, registered under the Government of India, including MSME and the Ministry of HRD. The Katlicherra Branch offers a range of computer education programs, including PG-DCSA, PG-DIT, C++, HTML, JAVA, CSS, TALLY-GST, DIT, DCOM, DCA, ADCA, DCAP, DFA etc. aimed at enhancing students' technical skills and employability .


Mission and Vision

The institute's mission is to provide quality-based education to make students employable, improve personality and skills through additional classes, and offer a wide range of well-researched industry-related courses .
